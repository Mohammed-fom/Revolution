About Us

Building Excellence Since 2010

Revolution Roofing & Construction is a premier construction company dedicated to delivering exceptional quality and innovative solutions for both residential and commercial projects.

With over a decade of experience in the industry, we have built a reputation for reliability, precision, and attention to detail. Our team of skilled professionals is committed to transforming your vision into reality.

We believe in building lasting relationships with our clients through transparent communication, timely project completion, and superior craftsmanship.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional construction services that exceed client expectations through innovation, quality craftsmanship, and unwavering commitment to safety and sustainability.

Our Vision

To be the leading construction company recognized for transforming spaces and building communities through excellence and integrity.

Our Team

Experienced Professionals

Our team consists of skilled craftsmen, engineers, and project managers with years of experience in the construction industry.

Quality Assurance

We maintain the highest standards of quality in every project, ensuring durability, safety, and client satisfaction.

Client-Centric Approach

We prioritize our clients' needs and work closely with them throughout the project to ensure their vision is realized.

Our Services

Roofing

Professional roofing services including installation, repair, and maintenance for residential and commercial properties. We work with various materials including shingles, metal, and flat roofs.

Commercial Construction

Complete commercial construction services from design to completion for offices, retail spaces, and industrial facilities. We handle projects of all sizes with precision and efficiency.

Residential Construction

Custom home building, renovations, and additions tailored to your lifestyle and preferences. We create spaces that reflect your personality while ensuring functionality and comfort.

Home Interior

Interior design and remodeling services to create beautiful, functional living spaces. From kitchen and bathroom renovations to complete home makeovers, we bring your vision to life.

Plumbing & Electrical

Professional plumbing and electrical services including installation, repair, and maintenance. Our certified technicians ensure all work meets safety standards and building codes.

Paving Services

Driveway, parking lot, and pathway paving services using high-quality materials and techniques. We create durable and aesthetically pleasing surfaces that withstand weather and heavy use.

Other Services

Painting

Professional interior and exterior painting services for residential and commercial properties. We use high-quality paints and techniques to deliver flawless finishes that enhance your space.

Site Management

Comprehensive project and site management to ensure your construction project runs smoothly from start to finish. We coordinate all aspects of the project to meet deadlines and budgets.

BOQ (Bill of Quantities)

Although not formally certified, through many years of experience in the construction industry, we can prepare accurate and detailed Bills of Quantities for your projects, ensuring proper budgeting and resource allocation.
